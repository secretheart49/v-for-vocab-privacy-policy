Cloud Backup and Data Deletion

Effective date: June 15, 2026

This page applies to V for Vocab: Learn Vocab Daily (com.vocabdaily.app), published by Semsso.

V for Vocab does not create a username/password account. The main learning features and Premium purchase can be used without creating an app account.

If you turn on Premium Cloud Backup, you sign in with Google on Android or Apple on iOS only to confirm that the cloud backup belongs to you. Your backup is stored by V for Vocab, not in Google Drive or iCloud Drive.

What Cloud Backup Stores

Cloud Backup stores compact learning progress and settings only. This may include your Google email or Apple email/private relay email, Firebase user ID, word IDs, learned/practice/mastered status, counts, quiz cycle status, timestamps, daily goal, selected topics, reminder settings, theme, and favorites.

We do not upload dictionary definitions, examples, synonyms, pronunciation text, or the full word catalog as part of your personal backup.

Delete Cloud Backup In The App

  1. Open V for Vocab.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Open Cloud Backup.
  4. Choose Delete Cloud Backup.
  5. Confirm the warning.

Your progress stays on your device. Your cloud backup is scheduled for permanent deletion after 72 hours. The app shows the exact deletion deadline in your local time.

If you reconnect Cloud Backup before the deadline, the scheduled deletion can be cancelled.

If You Cannot Access The App

Email us from the Google or Apple account used for Cloud Backup and request deletion of your V for Vocab Cloud Backup data.

articlespinnerai@gmail.com

Please include the app name, V for Vocab: Learn Vocab Daily, and the app identifier, com.vocabdaily.app.

Premium And Purchases

Deleting Cloud Backup does not cancel your Google Play or App Store subscription. You can manage or cancel subscriptions through your Google Play account on Android or Apple account subscriptions on iOS.

Purchase and subscription records may be retained by Google Play, Apple, and RevenueCat as needed for billing, entitlement management, fraud prevention, legal compliance, and support.
